Privacy Policy for ClearPage
Last Updated: January 23, 2026

1. Introduction
ClearPage is committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Information Collection and Use
Personal Data: ClearPage does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (such as names, email addresses, or browsing history) to any external servers.

Cookies: This extension uses cookies solely for the purpose of maintaining user preferences and ensuring the technical functionality of the new tab page. These cookies are processed locally and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

Local Storage: All user customizations and settings are stored locally on your device using the browser's internal storage API.

3. Third-Party Services (Donations)
ClearPage includes a link to Buy Me a Coffee for users who wish to support the project voluntarily.

We do not collect or process your financial information (credit card details, billing address, etc.).

All payment transactions are handled securely by Buy Me a Coffee. Please refer to their respective Privacy Policy for information on how they manage your data.

4. Data Security
Since no data is collected or stored on our servers, your information remains entirely within your own browser environment. We follow industry-standard practices to ensure the extension operates securely.

5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately.
